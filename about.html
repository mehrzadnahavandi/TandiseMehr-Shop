/ our history

TandiseMehr is a boutique interior design studio based in IRAN-Raht. It was established in 2013 by Mehrzad and Mehdi. We have an abundance of experience curating stunning interiors, from compact apartments to family homes in Golsar. Our interiors simultaneously enhance the architecture of the space and mirror the values of our clients. As a boutique studio, we can provide each customer with a completely personalized service that meets their every need. Our interior is designed according to the customer's needs at the forefront, executed flawlessly with the best attention to detail. We work with our clients to bring the most beautiful views to life, with the end result of an endless haven that complements even the busiest urban lifestyle.
